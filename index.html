How I got into coding..

During lockdown in March 2020, all children including my girls were staying at home, missing school and missing friends. I started an online Code Club after completing online training by Raspberry Pi Foundation and teaching myself the basics. They responded really well and enjoyed learning through play. I started a new journey into the coding world!
